Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp red chili flakes (optional for spice)
  • Salt (for pasta water)
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

Method

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and cook until golden (2-3 minutes).
  3. Add the red chili flakes (if using) and cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it directly to the pan. Toss to coat in the garlic oil.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley.
