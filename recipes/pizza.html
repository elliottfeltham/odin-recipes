Margherita Pizza

Recipe

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Toppings

  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil (for drizzling)

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add water and olive oil. Mix into a dough.
  3. Roll out the dough into a circle and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread tomato sauce on the dough, top with mozzarella, and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  5. Add fresh basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil before serving.
