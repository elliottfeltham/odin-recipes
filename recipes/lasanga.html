Lasanga

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked)
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara or pasta sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg (optional, for a firmer ricotta layer)
  • Salt & pepper (optional, for seasoning the beef)

Method

  1. In a pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Season with salt and pepper, if desired. Drain any excess grease, then stir in the jar of marinara sauce. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix the ricotta (or cottage cheese) with the egg (optional, but helps it firm up).
  3. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Layer 3 cooked lasagna noodles on top. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles. Sprinkle 1/3 of the shredded mozzarella on top. Spoon 1/3 of the meat sauce on top.
  5. Repeat this process 2 more times (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, meat sauce). Finish with a final layer of noodles, the remaining meat sauce, and sprinkle the Parmesan and any leftover mozzarella on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes before serving.
